## üí° How Organizations Should Use These Files

### Step 1: Download Templates
1. Download `system-users-import-template.csv`
2. Download `departments-and-job-titles-reference.csv`

### Step 2: Prepare Your Data
1. Open the reference file to see all job titles
2. Choose job titles that match your organization
3. Get your department IDs from the system
4. Fill the template with your employee data

### Step 3: Import
1. Upload via `/admin/system-users` ‚Üí "Import CSV"
2. Review results
3. Fix any errors and re-import if needed
